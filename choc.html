ABOUT US

                            Cadbury Dairy Milk is a British brand of milk chocolate manufactured by Cadbury.
                            It was introduced in the United Kingdom in June 1905 and now consists of a number
                            of products.

                            Every product in the Dairy Milk line is made with exclusively milk chocolate. 
                            In 1928, Cadbury's introduced the "glass and a half" slogan to accompany the
 
                            Dairy Milk chocolate bar,to advertise the bar's higher milk content.
